Privacy Policy for CommentCraft AI

Effective Date: November 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the "CommentCraft AI" Chrome Extension (the "Extension") collects, uses, and handles your data.

1. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary for the Extension to function:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (Email Address): We collect your Google Account email address (via chrome.identity.email) only when you choose to upgrade to our Premium version. This is necessary to create your license and link it to your account on our secure license server.
  • Authentication Data (API Keys): If you choose to use your own API keys for OpenAI or Gemini, the Extension saves these keys only in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local). These keys never leave your computer and are never sent to our servers.
  • Website Content (Non-Personal): The Extension reads video metadata (title, tags, description) from your active YouTube tab. This information is used solely to provide context to the AI for generating relevant comments and tags. It is not stored.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of the Extension (AI generation, license verification).
  • To process your Premium upgrade by sending your email to our license server and our payment processor (Paddle).
  • To save your settings locally on your device.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not and will not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties.

  • Your email is shared only with our secure license server and our payment provider (Paddle) for the sole purpose of activating your license.
  • Your self-hosted API keys are never transmitted to us or any other party.
  • All other data (like video metadata) is used temporarily and is not stored or shared.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy in the future. We will notify you of any changes by publishing the new policy here.
